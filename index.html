Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

OnTime Chrome Extension collects minimal data to provide its core functionality:

  • Timer Settings: Your timer preferences, themes, and widget positions are stored locally in your browser
  • Usage Data: No personal information or browsing data is collected or transmitted

Data Storage

All your timer settings and preferences are stored locally using Chrome's storage API. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

OnTime may link to external services (like the Chrome Web Store for related extensions). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your locally stored data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store.
